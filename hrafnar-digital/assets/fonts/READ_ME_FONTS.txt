# Brand fonts go here

Place your licensed web-font files in this folder:

- gascogne-serial.woff2   (headlines / display — Typodermic)
- sarvatrik.woff2          (body / UI — Universal Thirst)

Then open index.html and uncomment the @font-face block near the top
of the <style> section. Until you add them, the site uses free
fallbacks (Fraunces + Hanken Grotesk) that visually approximate the
brand fonts so nothing looks broken.

These are commercial fonts — supply them only from your own valid
web licenses.
